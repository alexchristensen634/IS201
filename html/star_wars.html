Star Wars Trilogies Ranked

  1. The Original Trilogy (Episodes IV, V, and VI)

    • Classic and timeless story
    • Darth Vader's redemption arc is incredible
    • Ewoks

  2. The Prequel Trilogy (Episodes I, II, and III)

    • The amazing progression of Anakin into Darth Vader
    • This trilogy has the best lightsaber fights (see video below)
    • Unfortunately Jar Jar Binks is in them

  3. The Sequel Trilogy (Episodes VII, VIII, and IX)

    • The plot wasn't consistent across movies
    • They do have incredible VFX
    • Darth Sidious should have stayed dead
